SunDesign Manifolds First-run Production
Delivery and pricing
First-run pricing applies on the first order for SunDesign manifolds, covering nonrecurring costs. Upon request Sun rebates the difference between first-run and production pricing when at least ten identical manifolds are ordered within six months of the first order.
First-run pricing applies when standard delivery is requested:
  • Preliminary drawings will be provided within two weeks of receipt of order.
  • SunDesign manifolds will ship standard lead time after the approval of drawings.
Premium delivery may be available:
  • Contact manifold engineering for availability.
  • Coating option will add one week to delivery.
  • Premium Delivery Breakdown:
    1. A premium of 20% of first-run pricing applies when delivery requested is less than four weeks after receipt of order of approved drawings, or when delivery requested is less than standard lead time.
    2. A premium of 30% of first-run pricing applies when delivery requested is less than two weeks after receipt of order, or when delivery requested is less than one week after approval of preliminary drawings.
    3. A premium of 40% of first-run pricing applies when delivery requested is less than one week after receipt of order.
Standard pricing applies when delivery requested is at least four weeks after receipt of order.
Premium delivery is available:
  • A premium of 10% of production pricing applies when delivery requested is less than four weeks after receipt of order.
  • A premium of 20% of production pricing applies when delivery requested is three days or less after receipt of order.
Cancellation charges
Cancellation charges apply based on work completed:
  • Design complete: difference between first-run and production pricing for quantity of one.
  • Machining begun: design complete charge plus 50% of first-run pricing for quantity cancelled.
  • Assembly complete: 100% of first-run pricing for quantity cancelled.
  • First-run Inactivity Fee: If preliminary approval is not received within 90 days after design submission, Sun has the right to apply the design complete charge.
Cancellation charges apply based on work completed:
  • Machining begun: 50% of production pricing for quantity cancelled.
  • Assembly complete: 100% of production pricing for quantity cancelled.
